About Me

Growing up in California, I never got to really enjoy rain or most forms of weather. After going to Ohio for school and experiencing seasons (and a tornado or two) for the first time, I decided Southern California wasn't for me. Shortly after graduating in Ohio, I packed up everything I could fit into my car and set off for the Pacific North West. I made the move on one week's notice after getting a job in Portland doing Q.A. for a vitamin gummy factory. Needless to say, the PNW was the place for me! I loved the weather, the people, the camping and the hiking! The locale was a dream come true! Unfortunately, I soon realized I didn't want to continue analyzing gummies for the rest of my life and decided to move back to Southern California to obtain a more flexible career that will give me the freedom to explore my favorite places in the PNW. I am now seeking employment in the Pacific North West as web developer and would love to hear from you!

Long before I got my degree in Chemistry, I fell in love with coding. I have made games in Unity, automated my blinds with an Xbox Kinect, and am working on an Natural Language Processor that uses TV scripts to understand comedic dialogue. Continuing my education in web development has easily been the best decision of my life. Switching careers to web development is both exciting and invigorating and I look forward to working on a team and building something to be proud of.
