About Me

Growing up in the desert, I never got to really enjoy rain or most other forms of weather. After going to Ohio for school and experiencing seasons (and a tornado or two), I decided it wasn't for me. Shortly after graduating in Ohio, I packed up everything I could fit into my car and set off for the Pacific North West.

I made the move on two week's notice after getting a job in Portland doing Q.A. for a vitamin gummy factory. Needless to say, the PNW was the place for me! I loved the weather, the people, the camping and the hiking! City life was a dream come true! Unfortunately, I soon realized I didn't want to continue analyzing gummies for the rest of my life and decided to move home to obtain a more flexible career that would give me the freedom to explore my new favorite places. I am now seeking employment in the Pacific North West as front-end engineer and would love to hear from you!

Long before I got my degree in Chemistry, I fell in love with coding. I got started as a hobbyist making games in Unity, automating my blinds with an Xbox Kinect, and was recently working on a Natural Language Processor that analyzes sitcom scripts to hopefully, one day, tell jokes.

Ever the realist, I understood my hobbies weren't going to pay the bills so I tried to find something that could and went to school for chemistry. Unfortunately, I learned that the chemist career isn't as fluid (pardon the bad science jokes) as I liked and after speaking to a few people, I learned about web development and decided to give it a try. That's when I fell in love with making applications for the web and deploying them on my website. Continuing my education in web development has easily been the best decision of my life. Joining the web development workforce has both been exciting and invigorating and I look forward to working on a team with you and building something to be proud of.
